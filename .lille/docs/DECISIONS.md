# Lille Decision Log

Version: 1.0

# Purpose

This document records important strategic, product, design and technical decisions made during the development of Lille.

Every significant decision should be documented here together with the reasoning behind it.

The goal is to preserve context, avoid repeating discussions, and help both humans and AI understand why certain choices were made.

If a decision changes in the future, never delete the original decision.

Instead:

• Mark it as superseded.

• Record the new decision.

• Explain why it changed.

## 2026-07-09 — Pinterest Language

Status:
Active

Owner:
Founder

### Decision

Pinterest will be operated entirely in English.

### Reason

English provides the largest long-term audience and supports international growth, Google SEO and Pinterest search visibility.

### Alternatives Considered

• Norwegian Pinterest

• Mixed Norwegian and English

### Impact

All Pinterest boards, pins, titles, descriptions and blog articles intended for Pinterest should be written in English.

---

## 2026-07-09 — Visual Identity

Status:
Active

Owner:
Founder

### Decision

All Pinterest graphics should follow a consistent Lille visual identity.

### Reason

Consistency improves brand recognition and builds trust over time.

### Design Guidelines

• Warm cream backgrounds

• Sage green as primary colour

• Dusty rose as accent colour

• Premium Scandinavian design

• Soft natural lighting

• Real babies

• Minimal layouts

• Plenty of whitespace

• Consistent typography

• Correct Lille logo

### Impact

All future Pinterest graphics should follow these principles.

## 2026-07-09 — Content Philosophy

Status:
Active

Owner:
Founder

### Decision

Lille will always teach before selling.

### Reason

Helping parents creates trust.

Trust creates long-term growth.

### Impact

Every blog article, Pinterest pin, email and social media post should provide genuine value before promoting the app.
