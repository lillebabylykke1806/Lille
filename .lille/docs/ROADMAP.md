# Lille OS Roadmap

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

Lille OS is the intelligence layer behind the entire Lille ecosystem.

Its purpose is to make every decision, every piece of content, every workflow and every AI interaction more consistent, scalable and intelligent.

This roadmap is intentionally long-term.

The goal is not to complete it quickly.

The goal is to build an extraordinary system over time.
