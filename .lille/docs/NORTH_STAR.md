# Lille North Star

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the long-term direction of Lille.

Products change.

Features change.

Technology changes.

Our purpose does not.

Every strategic decision should move Lille closer to this North Star.

---

# Our North Star

Every parent deserves to understand their baby.

Lille exists to replace uncertainty with understanding.

We believe that confident parents raise calmer families.

Technology is simply the tool that helps make that possible.

---

# Why We Exist

The first years of parenthood are filled with questions.

Is my baby tired?

Hungry?

Overstimulated?

Growing?

Every day parents make hundreds of decisions while feeling exhausted and uncertain.

Lille exists to reduce that uncertainty.

Not by telling parents what to do—

but by helping them understand what their own baby is communicating.

---

# What We Build

We do not build sleep trackers.

We do not build feeding logs.

We do not build AI for the sake of AI.

We build confidence.

Everything else is simply a tool.

---

# Our Promise

Parents should never leave Lille feeling:

• judged

• guilty

• overwhelmed

Instead they should leave feeling:

• calmer

• more confident

• supported

• understood

---

# Our Long-Term Goal

To become the world's most trusted parenting platform during the first years of a child's life.

Not because we know every answer.

But because we help parents discover the answers that fit their own baby.

---

# Success

Success is not measured by downloads.

Success is not measured by subscriptions.

Success is measured by parents saying:

"I finally understand my baby."

When that happens, we have done our job.

---

# Every Decision

Whenever we build something new, we should ask:

Does this help parents better understand their baby?

If yes,

we continue.

If not,

we rethink it.

---

# Remember

We don't build features.

We build confidence.

We don't track babies.

We help parents understand them.

Everything else is secondary.
