# Lille DNA

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the identity of Lille.

Everything we create should feel like it comes from the same company.

If someone only reads this document, they should understand what Lille stands for.

---

# What is Lille?

Lille is an intelligent parenting platform that helps parents better understand their baby.

We combine science, observation and technology to help parents recognize patterns in sleep, feeding and baby cues.

Lille does not promise perfect babies.

Lille helps parents feel calmer, more confident and more connected to their child.

---

# Our Mission

To help every parent better understand their baby.

Not through rigid schedules or one-size-fits-all advice—

but by understanding each baby's unique rhythm.

---

# Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted companion for parents during the first years of life.

Lille should be the place parents turn to when they want understanding instead of uncertainty.

---

# Who We Build For

Parents of babies.

Especially first-time parents who want reassurance, knowledge and practical guidance without feeling judged.

We build for tired parents.

Busy parents.

Curious parents.

Parents who simply want to understand their baby a little better.

---

# What Makes Lille Different

Lille is not built around generic advice.

Lille is built around the individual baby.

Every child has their own rhythm.

Instead of telling parents what they "should" do, Lille helps them discover what works for their own baby.

---

# What Lille Is Not

Lille is not a sleep training app.

Lille is not a medical app.

Lille does not replace healthcare professionals.

Lille does not promise perfect sleep.

Lille does not tell parents they are doing something wrong.

---

# How Parents Should Feel

Every interaction with Lille should leave parents feeling:

• Calmer

• More confident

• Better informed

• Less overwhelmed

• More connected to their baby

Never more anxious.

Never judged.

Never guilty.

# Our Competitive Advantage

Most parenting apps give advice.

Lille helps parents discover patterns.

The app learns from each baby's own data.

Instead of replacing parental intuition, Lille strengthens it.

---

# Every Decision Should Support This

Whenever we build a new feature, publish a blog article, create a Pinterest pin or launch a marketing campaign, we should ask:

"Does this help parents better understand their baby?"

If the answer is yes, we are building Lille.

If not, we should rethink the decision.
